Cc Gulbrandsen's Bio

At Syracuse, I major in magazine, news, and digital journalism with a fashion milestone focus, and minor in information technology and management. I am interested in pursuing a career in digital fashion design where I can be creative and inspired by those around me.

I have been a writer for University Girl Magazine since my sophomore year and just started a position as VP of Social Media for NCSC. I have also done makeup for a Jerk Magazine photoshoot and would love to do more.

Personal Information

I will always love fashion and getting dressed up to try new restaurants with friends or just to stay at home and cook, but in my free time, I love being outdoors, reading a book on the beach, and going for a long nature walk. When the weather doesn’t permit being anywhere else but inside, I love to bake, scrapbook, or watch Gossip Girl (a classic for me), Breaking Bad (which I am yet to finish), or any cheesy rom-com (The Proposal gets me every time).

I used to want to be an editor for a big magazine–Vogue: the crème de la crème for any young fashion writer's dream–but sometimes describing the way fabric makes me feel isn’t as exhilarating as the creative high I get from experimenting with designs. College has taught me that I don’t need to know exactly what I want to do or how I will get there but following inspiration is the only way to get anywhere.

Skills I hope to use in a future profession:

  • graphic design
  • magazine writing
  • digital marketing
  • creative direction
